Higher or Lower

Will the next hidden card beat yours? Ace is low, King is high.

How to play

  • 1. You start with one face-up card and a row of face-down cards (5, 7, or 10, by difficulty).
  • 2. Guess if the next face-down card is higher or lower than your rightmost card. Each time you are right, that card is added to your row on the right.
  • 3. Card order is Ace (low) through King (high). Suits do not matter.
  • 4. If the next card is the same rank, it flips for free and joins your row. That does not add to your streak.
  • 5. A wrong guess shows the card, then after a short pause the game ends. Clear the whole row to win.
