How to Play

  • 1. You'll receive random numbers based on your chosen difficulty.
  • 2. Place each number into one of the 10 slots so that the numbers end up in ascending order.
  • 3. You can't move numbers once placed, so think carefully before you click a slot.
  • 4. If a new number can't fit into any slot without breaking the order, the game ends.
  • 5. Fill all 10 slots correctly to get a perfect 10/10 score!
