Mimi's Memory Chess

Answer math questions, match colors, and win circles!

How to Play

  • 1. Answer the math question correctly.
  • 2. Your answer maps to a color (see color key at top).
  • 3. Pick a circle to reveal its hidden color.
  • 4. If colors match, you win the circle and get another turn!
  • 5. If no match, the circle hides after 2 seconds and it's the next player's turn.
  • 6. Win by collecting the most circles (All Circles mode) or reaching the target first!
