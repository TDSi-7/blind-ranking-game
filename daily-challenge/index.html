Daily Challenge

One Blind Ranking puzzle per day. Same for everyone. Fastest times on the leaderboard.

How to play

  • 1. You’ll get 10 numbers (1–100) one at a time.
  • 2. Place each number in one of 10 slots so they end up in ascending order.
  • 3. You can move the last-placed number before clicking “Next number”.
  • 4. Fill all 10 slots to complete the challenge. Your time is recorded when you place the 10th number.
