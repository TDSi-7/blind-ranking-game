Codebreaker

Guess the hidden color sequence in 7 tries. Green = right place. Orange = right color, wrong place.

How to play

  • 1. Pick Easy, Medium, or Hard—the computer hides a sequence of that length.
  • 2. Tap colors to fill each slot left to right, then submit.
  • 3. Submit your guess. Green dots mean correct color and position; orange means right color, wrong slot.
  • 4. You have 7 attempts to crack the code. Colors may repeat!
