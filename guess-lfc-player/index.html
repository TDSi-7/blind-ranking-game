How to play

  1. You start with 100 points. The only thing shown at first is the player’s position (e.g. Goalkeeper, Defender, Midfielder, Forward), and the player can be from Liverpool, Man United, Man City, Chelsea, Arsenal, or Tottenham.
  2. Type a name and press Guess. Each wrong guess costs points: 5, then 4, then 3, then 2, then 1 (1st miss through 5th miss). You have 5 guesses per round.
  3. Clues are face-down cards—tap one to spend points and reveal it. The new Club clue costs 25 points and each clue can only be bought once per round.
  4. If you guess correctly, your score for that round is the points you have left (after wrong guesses and clues).
